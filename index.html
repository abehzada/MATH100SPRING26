    Study Tips:

  • Preview the material. Skim the lecture slides the day before class so you have a sense of the main ideas and structure of the lecture.
  • Actively annotate during lecture. Make sure you have access to a hard copy of the slides or a digital copy on a tablet or laptop so you can annotate them as I annotate in class. If you do not have access to the slides, it will be difficult to take effective notes. Actively taking notes leads to better understanding and retention than simply sitting and listening.

Extra Credit

Almost every pen-and-paper homework assignment includes some questions marked in bold. You will get extra credit if you come to any of my office hours and explain your solutions to those questions. For each successful presentation you will earn 1/2 point extra credit which will be added directly to your final grade.
